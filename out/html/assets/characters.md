# Character Descriptions for Profile Pictures

Store profile pictures in: `out/html/assets/pictures`

## Facilyn
Wise, patient guide who teaches the Holistic Management Framework. Calm, grounded presence with an approachable, mentor-like demeanor.

## The Player ("Me")
Neutral, adaptable learner representing the player. Keep the portrait open-ended and inclusive, suitable for anyone stepping into the role.

## The Stranger (Harbour Encounter)
Harbour local who introduces the polycrisis. World-worn but attentive, carrying a sense of urgency and lived experience.

## The Vendor (Harbour)
Practical water seller showing immediate scarcity and economic pressure. Weathered and resourceful, focused on day-to-day survival.

## The Drawer/Artist (Harbour)
Quiet observer who sketches organizational logos in dust to preserve memory. Reflective and creative, with a thoughtful gaze.

## Delivery Drones
Autonomous drone with a curious, slightly quirky personality. Light, utilitarian design that hints at repurposed behavior.

## Maintenance Robots
Functional service robot focused on maintenance tasks. Sturdy, methodical, and clinical in appearance.

## Post Robot
Persistent delivery robot stuck in routine. Slightly outdated or battered, conveying determination without context.
